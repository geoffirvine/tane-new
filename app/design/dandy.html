Dandy

Designer; Gary Pennington

Made; Allermuir

Previous Next

Designed and developed by Gary Pennington for Allermuir, the Dandy range comprises of an armchair and two seat sofa, with metal frame or full length arms. Allermuir is one of the foremost design led furniture manufacturers based in the UK with global distribution.

The Dandy range has been specified in many high profile interiors including all passport offices throughout the UK. The Dandy range is available from Allermuir UK and Zenith Australia.
