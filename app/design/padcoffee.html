Pad Coffee Table

Designer; Gary Pennington

Made; Chairbiz

Previous Next

The Pad range of coffee/drinks tables are inspired by nature, based on the organic shapes of lily pads. The collection consists of three tables of differing heights.

Pad is available for purchase exclusively from Chairbiz www.chairbiz.com.au

Contact Chairbiz for pricing information and quotation

Materials

Produced from fabricated solid metal rod frame, with laser cut tops. Available in a range of powdercoat colours or copper finish

Dimensions

3 heights 430mm, 510mm & 610mm
