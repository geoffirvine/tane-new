Jive

Designer; Gary Pennington

Made; Tane Furniture

Previous Next

Jive is a contemporary collection of soft seating, comprising of a single arm chair and two seat sofa. The range is suitable for both commercial and residential applications. Jive is designed and made in Australia by Tane Furniture, using locally sourced materials and labour.

The Jive collection would make a striking statement in many environments from hotel lobbies, commercial spaces to modern residential dwellings.

Contact us for pricing information and quotation; design@tanedesign.com

Materials

The range is produced from FSC (Forest Stewardship Certificate) plywood carcass and is upholstered in Instyle Atlas, Eco-wool
