Groove Floor Lamp

Designer; Gary Pennington

Made; Tane Furniture

Previous Next

Description

The Groove floor lamp is a unique and elegant space saving design. Featuring unique hand printed lamp-shades by Ink & Spindle, with three options of pattern and colour. The design also includes an FSC accredited bamboo frame, with in-built coffee table, all hand-finished with natural oils.

The lamp is designed and manufactured using three different suppliers from the same collaborative space in Melbourne’s inner north-west. Including Tane Furniture (design & manufacture), Ink & Spindle (lamp shades) and Like Butter (CNC frames).
